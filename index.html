Tired of losing your keys, umbrella, your gloves or ... even your pet? AlmostLost is the solution to your headaches. AlmostLost is a solution based on RFID tags and smartphone application that will alert you when you walk away more than a certain distance from your precious objects. AlmostLost is easy to attach to your objects and even easier to use on your smartphone. What are you waiting ...? Through a small donation you can enter the draw for the first 100 AlmostLost to be manufactured. Forget about the bad times once and forever!

The iAlmostLost will also help you locate where was the last time your prized object was detected. With its integrated navigation system with Google Maps, you can retrace your steps to reach it. Magic?, No. Cutting edge technology making your service. It's amazing the amount of embarrassing situations in which AlmostLost may help. On the way to work, in the pool, at the supermarket ... at all times AlmostLost monitors the distance that you are your most beloved objects and let you know if you get away from them. In the worst case, will help you return to them.Trust us, make your donation!

FAQ

What does RFID stand for?
Radio-frequency identification (RFID) is the wireless non-contact use of radio-frequency electromagnetic fields to transfer data, for the purposes of automatically identifying and tracking tags attached to objects. The tags contain electronically stored information.
What's Google Maps?
Google Maps (formerly Google Local) is a web mapping service application and technology provided by Google, that powers many map-based services, including the Google Maps website, Google Ride Finder, Google Transit and maps embedded on third-party websites via the Google Maps API.
